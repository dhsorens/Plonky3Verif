## Invocation

`--preset aeneas` is required (else "Hash-consing key not found … serialization
mismatch between Rust and OCaml"). Each crate uses an isolated, per-crate
`CARGO_TARGET_DIR` so a cached `p3-field` cannot mask the clean-build panic.
Driven by [`run-extract.sh`](run-extract.sh). Logs preserved under
`/tmp/aeneas-extract/<crate>/` (`charon.log`, `aeneas.log.clean`, `lean/`, `*.llbc`).
